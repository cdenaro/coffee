Expresiones Bourbon Rojo

Finca La Sirena · Calarcá, Quindío · Bourbon Rojo Variety · Osmotic Dehydration with Watermelon

$26

A truly innovative coffee from producer Juan David Puerta at Finca La Sirena in Calarcá, Quindío. This Bourbon Rojo is processed using osmotic dehydration with fresh watermelon — fermented with watermelon, washed, and dried under UV light. The result is a cup unlike anything you've tasted: layers of tropical fruit and chocolate with an unmistakable silky texture.

Tasting Notes

Tropical fruits, cinnamon, flowers, and chocolate. Juicy citric acidity reminiscent of blueberry. Silky mouthfeel with a lingering finish of hazelnut cream and watermelon.

Brew Guide

Pour-over recommended at 192°F. Weigh your beans. Take your time.

Producer: Juan David Puerta. Finca La Sirena, Calarcá, Quindío. Direct and fair trade. Zero pesticides. Regenerative farming.
