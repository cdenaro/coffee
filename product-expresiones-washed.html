Expresiones Washed

Huila · 1,750m · Light Roast · Washed

$24

From our partners at Expresiones in Huila, this washed-process coffee showcases everything that makes Colombian high-altitude arabica extraordinary. By removing all of the fruit before drying, the washed process lets the bean's true character shine — clean, bright, and unmistakably complex. Grown at 1,750 meters alongside companion plants and native trees using zero pesticides. Each lot is a deliberate expression of place and season.

Tasting Notes

Peach, caramel, citrus zest.

Brew Guide

Pour-over recommended at 192°F. Weigh your beans. Take your time.
