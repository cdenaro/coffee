San Alberto Black Swan (Cisne Negro)

Edición Excepcional N°2 · Variety: Geisha (predominant), Bourbon, Caturra · Natural Process · Hacienda San Alberto, Buenavista, Quindío · 1,800m · Medium Roast

$43

The product of more than six years of experimentation at Hacienda San Alberto. Exotic varieties including Geisha, Bourbon, and Caturra were cultivated in experimental plantations, and master taster Juan Pablo Villota created this assemblage to achieve something unprecedented — the Black Swan. Natural process with a predominance of Geisha. Limited edition of 2,000 units. Quintuple Selection process.

Tasting Notes

Ripe red berries, red wine, and cocoa. Juicy, intense, and vibrant.

Brew Guide

Pour-over recommended at 192°F. Weigh your beans. Take your time.

Hacienda San Alberto, Buenavista, Quindío. Direct and fair trade. Zero pesticides. Regenerative farming.
