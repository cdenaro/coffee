Expresiones Wush Wush

Finca La Sirena · Buenavista, Quindío · Wush Wush Variety · Natural Process · 168-Hour Fermentation · African Bed Dried

$28

Wush Wush is named after a region in southwestern Ethiopia where the variety has been cultivated for centuries among teas and spices. Known for its remarkable genetic stability, Wush Wush maintains well-defined flavor notes and aromatic structure that other varieties can lose over generations. Though grown in Colombia for over 30 years, its popularity has surged recently as producers discover its exceptional potential. This lot from Finca La Sirena undergoes 168 hours of natural fermentation and is dried on raised African beds, producing a sensorial profile that is fruity, floral, and irresistibly sweet.

Tasting Notes

Maple honey, ripe purple fruits, and spices. Fruity, floral, and sweet with defined aromatic structure.

Brew Guide

Pour-over recommended at 192°F. Weigh your beans. Take your time.

Producer: Juan David Puerta. Finca La Sirena, Buenavista, Quindío. Direct and fair trade. Zero pesticides. Regenerative farming.
