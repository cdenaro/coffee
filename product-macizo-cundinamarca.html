Macizo Cundinamarca Blend — Washed

Cundinamarca · 1,750m · Yellow Bourbon, Castillo, and Caturra · Washed

$24

A washed blend of three classic Colombian varieties — Yellow Bourbon, Castillo, and Caturra — grown at 1,750 meters in Cundinamarca. This blend showcases the balanced sweetness and complex acidity that makes this region exceptional. Scored 87 by the SCA.

Tasting Notes

Aroma of butter with a bit of spiciness. Sugar cane, nutty flavor, and plum aftertaste. Orange acidity. Caramel and brown sugar sweetness.

Brew Guide

Pour-over recommended at 192°F. Weigh your beans. Take your time.

Farmed organically with zero pesticides using regenerative principles. Direct and fair trade.
